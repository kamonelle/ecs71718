Family

I have a pretty interesting family environment. My dad is and immigrant from a Carribean island, Dominica. So his culture was very different than America's culture. Whenever my family would come over everyone was over having a good time. There was always great food, like fried fish and more. It was always a blast being with them.





My mom's side of the family is quite different. Her family is originally from Oklahoma. They have a reunion every year in May. It used to be fun when I was little, but now not so much. All my grandma and my mom do is shop, and at times it's boring. However, I do like spending time with my family.
